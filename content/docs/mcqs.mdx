## 2. Profitability Index (PI) is calculated as:

**Answer: A. PV of inflows / Initial investment**

- **A. PV of inflows / Initial investment**
- B. Profit / Cost
- C. Revenue / Expense
- D. Cost / Revenue

Formula:

$$
PI=\frac{\text{Present Value of Future Cash Inflows}}{\text{Initial Investment}}
$$
